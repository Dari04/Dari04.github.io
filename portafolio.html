For this challenge, the students work in teams of two people, in my case my partner was Karla Ríos; The challenge was to layout the landing page as it was designed in a pdf, but also add the responsive view for all devices, that was what cost us the most since we still did not learn how to achieve that, that's when we exercise our self-taught institute and we met bootstrap and materialize; In that opportunity we use materialize.
